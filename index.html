Yi-Chien Lin

林依蒨
Google Scholar ↗ GitHub ↗ LinkedIn ↗

I was born and raised in Taiwan. I grew up in Hsinchu, a windy city where bangs and umbrellas don't function properly. I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Linguistics at the Ohio State University, working with William Schuler and Michael White. My research interests lie in the computational modeling of human sentence processing and natural language structures. I am specifically interested in adopting cognitively/linguistically motivated methods in studying these areas.
